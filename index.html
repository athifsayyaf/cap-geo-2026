About the Program

What is CAP-GEO?

A flagship intensive training program at the intersection of geophysics, remote sensing, and earth observation.

CAP-GEO is a three-day intensive training program designed to disseminate practical knowledge in advanced Remote Sensing and Geophysical Imaging. We bring together experts, engineers, and researchers to master integrated investigation methods for hazard assessment and resource mapping.

The workshop bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and field-ready skills. Participants gain direct, hands-on experience with state-of-the-art instruments including UAV-LiDAR systems, terrestrial laser scanners, GNSS rovers, ground-penetrating radars, and InSAR processing platforms.

CAP-GEO 2026 is designed for postgraduate and doctoral scholars, faculty, field geologists, and working professionals seeking to integrate surface and subsurface mapping techniques into their research or professional practice.
